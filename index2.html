Halloween Scare Cinema

Immersive Cinema Experience, 25th - 28th October 2018

The Original Scare Cinema

Live Scare Maze - Pop Up Cinema - Street Food - Horror Bar

This Halloween we have opened the doors to one of Glasgow's most haunted venues for a blood curdling cinema experience which will devour the weak, engulf the fearful and break even the strongest of wills. Set in an old abandoned warehouse you will need to brave the maze of terror which is the home to some very unfriendly inhabitants who are dying to meet you. Once inside the screaming room take your seat but don’t get too comfortable as the fun has only just begun.

Guests can expect to come face to face with some of their favorite horror movie characters and take part in a spooky ritual summoning the ghost of one of Glasgow’s most gruesome serial killers the notorious Dr Henry Fergus more commonly known as the Glasgow Ripper.

Survivors can enjoy a range of devilish good cocktails, wines and spirits at our terror bar and feast on home made street food.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Is A Scare Cinema?

If you love Halloween, love movies and love to be scared then you will LOVE our scare cinema. Expect a great movie with some spooky surprises!

Will I be really Scared?

We hope so - It's all about Halloween fun!

Where Is The Cinema

The cinema is being held at a haunted warehouse just seconds away from the BBC HQ facing the SEC Hydro. It's super easy to access via bus, taxi, tube or car with plenty of parking around the area.

Is Food And Drink Available?

Yes we have a selection of street food for you to enjoy during your time at the festival. All our menus include vegetarian and vegan options. We also have a fully stocked Horror Bar

What Should I Wear?

The event is talking place in an old warehouse which can get cold. We recommend dressing with warm clothing and flat shoes.

Can children attend?

We have 2 family friendly screenings which are suitable for older children. Still expect plenty of scares and fun with our characters.

I Can’t Find My Ticket Email?

Please check your spam folder as sometimes tickets can end up in the wrong folder. If you have accidently deleted them or can’t find them in your spam inbox please message us using Facebook at https://www.facebook.com/HorrorNights.co.uk
